Crazy painter

A Portable LASER ENGRAVER

Designer

XuYunxiu, LiXinghan, LuoShan, ZhaoMi

Date

2018-2019

Category

Integrated

The thesis of this project is here:

A portable sketching robot based on artificial intelligence

Crazy Painter has the ability to engrave on various types of texture, like paper, leather, wooden wares. Unlike traditional laser engravers, we designed a parallelogram mechanism structure which can significantly reduce the volume and the power of the machine as you can keep the robot into your pocket.

A software based on QT was designed for the robot, it can slice the given images and convert them to the instruction of the robot. The instruction codes will send by BLE. We also designed a Hand-Drawing correct system based on convolution network which can correct stick figure you write automatically.
